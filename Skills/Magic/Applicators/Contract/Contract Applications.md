## Duration

The contract allows an effect to last for the listed duration, or until the contract is broken.  

|Contract Power|Duration|
|---|---|
|1|1 Round|
|2|1 Minute|
|3|10 Minutes|
|4|1 Hour|
|5|8 Hours|
|6|24 Hours|
